Raghu Raman is a Tamil Actor specializing in wearing his heart on his sleeve and braving the unknown. As a performer, he is widely dedicated to being in the moment, always ready for whatever is coming next. Originally from Puducherry, IN, Raghu graduated from the University of Pondicherry with B.Tech, and shortly after headed for acting. With a strong background and passion for acting, Raghu found a home on one of Chennai's most provocative and beloved youtube channels- Madras Central. He has acted in numerous shows for the company. Recently Raghu took his passion for acting to the screen.
